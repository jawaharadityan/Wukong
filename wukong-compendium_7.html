Combat Foundation

Choose Your Stance

Every boss strategy below assumes you can flow between these three. Tap one to see how it shapes your approach to every fight on this page.

Smash

Highest per-hit damage. Breaks posture in 2–3 charged hits.

Pillar

Plants the staff — fight with acrobatic kicks. Best mobility & vault dodges.

Thrust

Fast pokes that build toward a guaranteed-hit finisher. Great vs. shielded foes.

Smash Stance — the default recommendation throughout this guide. Charge attacks paired with Cloud Step (dodge-cancel into a charged hit) carry you through almost every fight on this list except Erlang and the Stone Monkey, who punish the read.
